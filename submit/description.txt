Simple Core Metrics is a minimalistic watch face that presents essential daily metrics in a structured and easy-to-read format. It provides the following information:

Date: Keep track of the current day at a glance.
Unread Notifications Count: Know when there are pending notifications.
Battery Status: Displays battery percentage and estimated days remaining.
Weather Insights: See min-max temperature (°C/°F based on settings), "feels like" temperature, and precipitation chance.
Phone Connection Indicator: Shows only when your phone is disconnected.
Time Display: Clear and precise timekeeping.
Alarm Indicator: Shows only when an alarm is set.
Activity Tracking: Monitor calories burned and distance traveled (km/mi based on settings).
Heart Rate Monitor: Displays your current heartbeat for real-time tracking.
